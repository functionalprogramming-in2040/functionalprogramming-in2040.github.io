    There are 5 obligs + one set of exercises at the end which is optional. The tasks are handed out in intervals of 2 weeks, and for each there is approximately 2 weeks for solving them. The obligs are made available at the github-repos h25, i.e., here. See also the info in vortex.

    See also IFI’s rules on obligs.
