    The lecture and its material is parcelled up in “weeks’’. Each calendar week covers one portion of the material, but there may be one or two weeks during autumn without an actual lecture, maybe falling on a holiday. In other words, what is called ``week 7’’ here may factually not be presented in the 7th calendar week after the first lecture. In the overview, the slides, etc. when we refer to ``week x’’ we mean the corresponding portion of the material, not the calendar week in the semester.

    Also the exercises and group sessions follow that structure, and the exercises labelled ``week x’’ will typically not covered in ``week x’’, but the week afterwards.
